About me

I am a PhD student in Mathematics at the Université de Sherbrooke, under the supervision of Maxence Mayrand.

Before that, I was a PhD student at the Université Sidi Mohamed Ben Abdellah, supervised by Lahcen Oukhtite.

Research Interests

My research focuses on advanced topics in differential and algebraic geometry, including derivations theory, algebraic geometry, Lie groups and Lie algebroids, topological quantum field theories, shifted symplectic geometry, and Poisson structures.
